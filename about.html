About us

Located just outside the bustling city limits,Sarang Hotel is a tranquil haven offering a perfect blend of relaxation and luxury.Nestled amidst lush greenery and expansive grounds,this idyllic hotel provides a serene escape from the everyday hustle and bustle.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful getaway,a romantic escape,or a rejuvenating vacation,Sarang Hotel provides a delightful combination.It is the perfect destination for those looking to unwind and reconnect with nature while enjoying the luxuries of a well-appointed hotel.

The hotel boasts a range of comfortable and well-appointed accommodations to suit every guest's needs.A stunning swimming pool that serves as a refreshing oasis for guests.The onsite restaurant offers a delightful culinary journey for its guests,with a focus on using locally sourced ingredients.A well-stocked and meticulously curated selection of beverages,including a wide range of wines,spirits,cocktails and non-alcoholic options.
